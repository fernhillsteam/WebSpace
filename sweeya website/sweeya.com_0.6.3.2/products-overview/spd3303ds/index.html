SPD3303D/S Programmable Linear DC Power Supply
Main Features and Benefits:
  • Separate control and independent triple outputs: 30V/3A*2, 2.5V/3.3V/5V/3A*1, total 195W powers
  • 5 digits voltage and 4 digits current display, min resolution: 1mV, 1mA
  • Three output modes: independent, series and parallel connect; support timing output, waveform display and SCPI remote command
  • 4.3 inches TFT-LCD display with 480*272 high-resolution
  • 100V/120V/220V/230V compatible design, to meet the need of different grid
  • Smart temperature controlled fan, effectively reduce the noise
  • Save/Recall 5 group system specifications, support data storage expansion
